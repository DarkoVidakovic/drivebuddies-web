The practical driving test is an exciting moment for many — the nerves are real, and a lot rides on it. But with the right tips you can go into the exam calmly and significantly improve your chances of passing.

## Start Relaxed

Before we get to the tips, the most important thing: examiners want to see that you drive safely, anticipate hazards, and stay calm — not that you're perfect. Keep your eye scanning, mirror–shoulder check, and speed adaptation under control, signal clearly, and take your time with clean parking. With that mindset, you'll read the following points as a checklist for confident driving rather than a source of stress.

## 5 Tips for a Relaxed Führerprüfung

### 1. Practice Makes Perfect

Drive as often as possible in varied situations: city, motorway, country roads, rain, and darkness. The wider your experience, the more confident you'll feel.

### 2. Know the Test Routes

Talk to your Fahrlehrer about typical exam routes. If you've already driven the area, navigation is one less thing to think about.

### 3. Stay Composed

Examiners don't focus on small mistakes — they look at how you drive overall. If a small slip happens, just keep going.

### 4. Prepare Well

Get enough sleep, eat something light beforehand, and arrive at the test centre early. That way you start the exam relaxed and focused.

### 5. Trust Your Fahrlehrer

Your Fahrlehrer knows exactly when you're ready to take the test. Take their tips seriously — they're invaluable.
